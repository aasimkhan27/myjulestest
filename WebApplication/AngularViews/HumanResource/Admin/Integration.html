Payroll Apps

This integration syncs the following information:
  • Employees are imported from your Gusto account and any changes to their profiles are synced to Wenodo.
  • Wages are imported from your Gusto account and any changes to your locations are synced to Wenodo.
You can export the following information with this integration:
  • Timesheets can be exported from your Wenodo account to your Gusto account to process payroll.
