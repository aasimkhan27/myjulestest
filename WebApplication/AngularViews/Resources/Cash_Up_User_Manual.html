What should your cash handling procedures include?

What are the prerequisites of a cashing up process?

Prerequisites

  1. Ensure, all terminals are connected and synced successfully with a back-office server or cloud.
  2. Ensure, all the transactions/checks are closed.
  3. Ensure, there are no open or transitory checks. (Important!)
  4. Ensure, you have all the PDQ terminals in one place.
  5. Ensure you have all the petty cash receipts with you. What are the reports required for a cashing up process?

Reports:

  1. Print POS Z-reports from consolidated form, where you can print one report which adds up all the POS terminals.
  2. Print Z-reports from all the PDQ-terminals.
  3. Identify and keep all the complementary, voids, deposit received/redeemed, vouchers sold or redeemed, delivery partner, and account customer checks on one side. (in separate piles)

Log In:

how to log-in to the cash-up app?

  1. Launch app.wenodo.com & Log-in with your credentials.
  2. From the App-Suite, click on Cash-up App
  3. Once you are in the App, select the Cash-up list, from the left panel.

Begin the Cash-up process: by clicking

Cash-up Entry Stage:

  1. Choose Session – Lunch, Dinner or All

    Depending on the set-up you can choose the relevant session to begin the EOD process.


  2. Choose Session – Lunch, Dinner or All
    • If the API is available, sales data will automatically appear once you chose the correct session, you can also refresh the data by clicking the refresh button.
    • If the API is not available, please download the pre-defined sales report format and save it at your local drive and upload the report by choosing the button.

Float:

Please count the float first and enter the counts against the cash denominations, ensure the Variance is ZERO or minimum.

Cash:

Count the cash and balance the Epos cash figure, ensure the variance is close to zero. Use the Notes section to explain the reasons for any cash variances.

Cards:

  • Please select the right PDQ Terminal from the dropdown list. Select the right location from the dropdown list.
  • Choose the correct Authentication Code and complete the Amex, Mastercard, Visa and Others entries.
  • You can add any many lines you may require by clicking the button.

Petty Cash:

From the petty cash receipts, start entering each expense individually.

  • Paid to – supplier name.
  • Category (drop down) – choose correct cost category.
  • Goods- describe what is bought i.e., veg, cleaning spray, etc.
  • Gross- total amount
  • Approver – select the approver from the dropdown.
  • Vat Amount- enter vat amount as displayed on the receipt.
  • Net Amount – enter the net amount.
  • Upload – upload image if possible

Delivery:

If you have any account customer or invoice later, please use this section.

  • Company Name – your delivery partner name
  • Units- no. of checks from that delivery partner.
  • Date- select the date of delivery.
  • Payment Method- select the payment mode from the dropdown, or enter how it is paid.
  • Amount- summation of all the checks from the mentioned delivery partner. It is advisable to enter individual checks if the volume is manageable.

Account Customer:

If you have any account customer or invoice later please use this section.

Please complete the entries.

  • Invoice# – enter the invoice no. or check no.
  • Customer Name – enter the guest or host name.
  • Company- if you have any pre-defined account.
  • Amount – enter the total amount.
  • Payment Method- select the payment from the dropdown.
  • Notes- please enter any notes with as much info as possible.

Voucher Issued:

  • Voucher Type- from the drop-down list please select pre-define or
  • Voucher#- for pre-defined, the numbers are auto-generated, for an open voucher you have to originate your own voucher number i.e. Comp, Goodwill, Charity, Event, etc.
  • Amount- sale value of the voucher.
  • Validity- select the validity date or expiry date.
  • Sold to- customer name.
  • Payment method- how it’s paid? Select from the dropdown.
  • Invoice#- invoice number if available.
  • Notes-please makes any notes if you require.

Voucher Redeemed:

  • Voucher#- please enter the correct voucher number, the data will appear automatically. To do this first you need to create the voucher by following the step number 7.
  • Amount- auto.
  • Validity- auto.
  • Redeemed by- enter who has redeemed the voucher?
  • Payment Method- select from the dropdown.
  • Invoice#- invoice number if available.
  • Notes-please makes any notes if you require.

Deposit Received:

  • Invoice#- if you take deposits on POS please enter the invoice number, else leave it blank.
  • Deposit Ref- self-created ref number initial of FIRST & LAST NAME followed by DOB or Memorable Date DDMMYYYY.
  • Amount-value of deposit
  • Date- date of party or future date when the deposit will be redeemed.
  • Payment Method-how it’s paid, select from the dropdown.
  • Name of Payee-payee name or customer name.
  • Notes-please makes any notes if you require, i.e. any requirements, dietary instructions, decoration, cake, etc.

Deposit Redeemed:

  • Invoice#- please entre invoice number.
  • Deposit Ref- type Deposit Ref# i.e. FLDDMMYYYY. [F: First Name; L: Last Name (initial only)].
  • Value-auto.
  • Date- auto.
  • Payment Method- auto.
  • Name of Payee- enter the payee name.
  • Notes-please makes any notes of any feedback or if any other person has redeemed the deposit on Payee’s behalf.

Complimentary:

Voids:

If the value of the void does not appear automatically, please count the total Qty & Amounts by each reason and complete the entries.

Complimentary:

Please key in each complementary check# individually. As a best practice, all the comps shall be re-approved by the senior management.

If the comp is given to compensate for a complaint or unpleasant experience you can use the bottom notes section for further explanation.

  • Authorized by-name of the individual who has approved the complimentary. Use the notes section for more details.
  • Check#- please key check number.
  • Table# – please key table number.
  • Reason of comp- please select the most relevant reason from the drop-down list.
  • of covers- very critical.
  • Time- ket in time on the check# (optional).
  • Amount- Complementary value.
  • Notes- please mention the name of the customer.

Review:

Before submitting the final numbers, you can review each session or ALL section, at this point you can you back by clicking the previous button and edit or correct the entries.

After clicking the submit button you will not get any opportunity to correct anything, you can also make notes of the overall service in the bottom notes section.

What happens once the cash-up is submitted?

The cash-up of the day’s status will change from pending or draft to submitted.

The Manager/Admin will see the cash-up of the day under his approved section, Manger/Admin will be able to review the cash-up, can correct or edit any step, and can also make additional comments before approving/submitting it.

Upon submission, selected reports will automatically be sent via emails to pre-defined recipients.
