KP / AGNENCY STAFFS

  • Create a department for KP/Agency Staffs in Rota system.
  • Rota/Schedule should be created for each of the Agency/KP and approve each week on the Rota system.
  • Need to discuss – Cross verification of Agency time sheet with Rota system, who will be doing this (Client or Wenodo).
  • Invoices/Times sheet should be verified with rota before sending to the Wenodo.
  • Agree with the supplier to send the invoices on weekly basis.
  • Any dispute should be communicated with Wenodo to stop the payments for the invoices.
  • Make a note on the Invoice/Timesheet if required inform the designated personnel/service provider.
  • All the invoices should be sent to Wenodo via scanning system in place.

REGULAR

  • All the invoices should be verified against the approved Purchase Orders/work orders.
  • Verified invoices can be posted to the Xero.
  • Any invoices which do not agree to Purchase Orders should be shared with the client for approval.
  • All the invoices should be validated against the Purchase Orders/work orders as a weekly task to avoid mismatch at the month end.
